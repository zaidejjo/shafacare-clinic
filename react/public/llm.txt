# Shafa Care Clinic

## About
Shafa Care Clinic is a modern, family-focused medical clinic based in **Amman, Jordan**. Founded and led by **Dr. Firas Ajo**, the clinic delivers compassionate, patient-centered healthcare with a full spectrum of services under one roof.

## Core Pages
- **Home** — `/` — Landing page with hero, services overview, doctor team, patient testimonials, FAQ, and call-to-action.
- **Services** — `/services` — Detailed listing of all 8 medical services.
- **About** — `/about` — Mission, values (Compassion, Excellence, Community, Innovation).
- **Contact** — `/contact` — Phone, WhatsApp, email, address, contact form, working hours.

## Medical Services
1. **General Medicine** — Annual physicals, chronic care, acute treatment.
2. **Pediatrics** — Newborn to adolescent care.
3. **Dental Care** — Cleanings, cosmetics, orthodontics.
4. **Cardiology** — Heart screenings, ECGs, consultations.
5. **Lab & Diagnostics** — On-site lab testing.
6. **Wellness & Vaccines** — Immunizations, nutrition, prevention.
7. **Mental Health** — Confidential counseling.
8. **Physiotherapy** — Rehabilitation and mobility programs.
